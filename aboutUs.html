Our Founders


Founded on the principles of universal love, equality, and selfless service, the Avadoot Saibaba Temple in Nandiyambakkam was born from the deep spiritual devotion and vision of Mrs. Bhuvaneswari and her late husband, Sri Venkatasubramanian. Driven by a lifelong desire to help others and a profound calling to spread Shirdi Sai Baba's message of peace, Mrs. Bhuvaneswari generously donated their own land and savings to lay the foundation. Locals recall a long-standing hearsay that several years ago, a noble person predicted a Sai temple would one day grace this site; today, it is widely believed that Mrs. Bhuvaneswari was the chosen vessel to carry out His divine will. With the ultimate blessings of Baba, alongside the heartfelt donations of family, friends, kind strangers, and the invaluable support of the local villagers, the temple successfully came into being in July 2017. Now managed by the Shri Shirdi Sai Avadhoot Spiritual Trust, the temple operates as a welcoming sanctuary for all, with Mrs. Bhuvaneswari overseeing its daily operations from Chennai to ensure it remains a pillar of positivity and unity for the entire community.
